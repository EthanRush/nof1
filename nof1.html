Projects

Notable projects: At Udemy, developed ‘the box,’ a system to set clear performance expectations, fairly evaluate contributions, and reward accordingly. For Google, a talent agency for VPs as part of the Google School for Leaders. At Ogilvy, designed and led Project X, an experiment that broke up business-as-usual, where small teams generated big ideas for Starbucks, Southwest, and Footlocker; established top team governance and enterprise operating principles for Johnson & Johnson’s med tech sector. At Leo Burnett, designed a concept for faster, cheaper, and happier creative teams; developed ‘Concept to Code’ (C2C) for PayPal, an executive experience to accelerate innovation. At Wharton, designed and managed executive education program for Dubai World, the largest at that point in Wharton history; launched an unconventional leadership campaign at J&J Pharmaceutical, focused on boosting leaders’ impact bringing differentiated drugs to market quickly; led an 8-year executive development program at Southern California Edison, preparing half the executive team, including the CEO.
